Knowledge is Game!

ReJOYCE Fundamentals Academy (RFA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting our youth on and off the court. Student athletes in our program develop those essential qualities that contribute to life-long success while becoming skilled basketball players and having fun!

Coach Walker and her team work with our youth to teach them the importance of perseverance, cooperation, friendship, healthy choices and serving others. Basketball is a powerful motivator to teach more than the game; we use it to teach success in life!
